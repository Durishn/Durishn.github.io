First and foremost, thanks for stopping by! I'm Nic, a Computer Scientist from Guelph, Ontario. Over the last decade I've dedicated myself to computational research and professional web development.

In that time I've had the honour and priviledge to work on some amazing projects, with inspiring individuals including: completing my MSc degree in Human-Computer Interaction from the University of Guelph, developing the eNuk application for the remote community of Rigolet, working on the RightMesh mobile-mesh network, speaking at over 12 conferences including g0v in Taipei, and redeveloping the City of Guelph website structure and theme. I have also received some notable accolades, such as receiving: the Young Innovator of Guelph Award, the SoCS Teaching Assistant Award of Excellence, and a full Graduate Tuition Scholarship to UoG

My curiosity drives me to this day, and I am still open to new projects. Whether to collaborate on a new idea or to help push along an established project, I'm at your service.
