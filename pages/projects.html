I built and loaded custom network monitors to log the internet around town, and modeled the affects of external variables on transfer speeds. Lawrence Assistant

As a computer scientist, I often get gifted various tech and 'Smart Home' devices. However I quickly got fed-up with the growing list of settings, apps, crappy API's and closed ecosystems which made connecting the growing varieties of devices nearly impossible. Lawrence Assistant is a custom built SmartHome system running on a Hass.io backend. This project has gotten a surpising amount of attention within the Home Assistant Forums and on Reddit.
